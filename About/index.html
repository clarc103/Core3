Welcome to "Crosswalk Portraits," a visual journey from "walk" to "stop," where each page subtly evolves from isolated walk signs to compelling full-bleed images. Page hues shift from pristine white to red, mirroring the transition from walking to waiting. Delve into the details: each information page connects page numbers with the image's perspective corner and the photographer's vantage point, capturing the essence of the crosswalk experience. Captivating image spreads introduce the vibrant individuals whose stories intersect at urban crossroads. Crafted with Moab covers, matte copy paper, and coptic binding, the book blends utility with artistry, featuring the classic "Interstate" typeface, evoking the spirit of American road signage. The covers tell their own story, inviting you to glimpse pedestrians' journeys from walk to stop. "Crosswalk Portraits" is a visual symphony and a compelling narrative celebrating moments in between.
